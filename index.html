The Calibration Game

Calibration game is a game to get better at identifying hallucination in LLMs.

This is a research artifact and intended for research and educational purposes only.

How to Play

Rate on a scale from 0 to 1 your confidence in the correctness of the response of a LLM to a question.

- A rating of 0 indicates you believe the answer to be absolutely incorrect.
- A rating of 1 indicates you believe the answer to be absolutely correct.

Calibration Score

At the end of the game, we obtain a score that indicates how far away your predictions are from a perfectly calibrated response.

- A perfectly calibrated response is one that correctly predicts the correctness of the LLM's answer in every single instance.
- Each session comprises 20 questions, and the best calibration score obtainable is 0.
